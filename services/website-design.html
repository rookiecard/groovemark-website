FAQs

Everything you’re wondering about your new website.

Three things. First, fixed pricing in writing before any work begins, no surprise invoices. Second, you own everything from day one: domain, code, hosting, analytics, all in your name, no platform lock-in. We can run it for you, but we don’t hold it hostage. Third, we turn down projects we don’t believe we can do well. If your business needs a refresh instead of a rebuild, we tell you on the call. If we’re not the right fit, we point you to someone who is.

A focused Starter site is typically ready in two to three weeks. A Growth site runs three to six weeks. Scale builds with custom integrations or multi-location architecture take longer because of the additional scoping and testing. Every project gets a specific, dated timeline in writing before we start, so the date you see is the date you can count on.

Only what you have. On the kickoff call we’ll send a short list of what we need from you (logos, photos, content you want featured) with a deadline. Send what you have by the deadline and we start. Missing something? We can source it, shoot it, or write it as an add-on, scoped on the call.

Most clients keep us on a light ongoing care plan. That covers updates, backups, security patches, and small edits in the background, without any ticket system to deal with. If something needs changing, you send one message and it gets done, typically same-day. If you’d rather own the site outright and manage updates yourself, we hand it over cleanly with a walkthrough so you know exactly how to run it. Care plan pricing scales to the site and is scoped on the first call.

Every site ships with the foundations both SEO and AEO reward: structured data, sub-one-second load times, mobile-first architecture, proper local schema, and the clear, citable content AI tools need to quote you as the answer. For less-competitive local categories, clients regularly land in the Google map pack inside 30 to 60 days. For competitive markets, plan for 90 days of consistent activity to see page-one movement. AI search visibility (ChatGPT and AI tools, Google’s AI Overviews) follows the same foundations, scoped to how often your industry gets asked about. On the first call we tell you specifically what’s realistic for your market, not a generic promise.

Every project includes design revisions: two rounds in Starter, three in Growth, five in Scale. We work with you until the design lands, then we build it. If after good-faith revisions you still aren’t happy, you get a full refund. That promise is in writing in the proposal.

You get a senior operator on every project, not a junior on an account team. You get fixed pricing in writing, not an hourly meter. You own the work outright, not as a hostage to a platform you can’t leave. And you talk directly to the person building the site, every time.
