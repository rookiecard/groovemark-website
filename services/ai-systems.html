Process

Three steps.
No friction. No surprises.

The same playbook every Groovemark build runs on. Each phase ends with a short Loom showing what shipped. Nothing goes live until you sign off.

Phase 01

Discover

You do A 45-minute call. Walk us through how leads come in today, and where they sit or slip.

Outcome:A scoped proposal with what we will build, what it will cost, and how much time you’ll get back.

Phase 02

Build

We do Configure the AI, train it on your business, connect it to your calendar and inbox, test it end-to-end.
You do Send us your top 30 customer questions. Twice-weekly check-ins to review the AI’s test conversations.

Outcome:A system that has handled 25+ test conversations on your watch before it ever talks to a real customer.

Phase 03

Launch + Tune

We do Go-live monitoring, weekly tuning of AI replies that miss, monthly performance reports with recommendations.
You do A 20-minute monthly call to review what is working and approve any wording or routing changes.

Outcome:A reliable system capturing every lead and request, even when you’re closed. You stop checking on it.
